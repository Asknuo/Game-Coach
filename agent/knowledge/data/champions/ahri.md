# Ahri - Mid Lane Guide

## Overview
Ahri is a mobile AP assassin-mage hybrid with strong pick potential and safe laning. Best played as a roamer and pick-maker in mid game.

## Early Game Strategy (0-14 min)
Focus on farming and poking with Q. Ahri has weak early kill pressure before level 6. Use Auto-Q-Auto to proc Electrocute for safe trades. Save E (Charm) for gank setup or defensive disengage — do NOT throw random charms. Ward one side of river and hug that side to avoid jungle ganks. At level 6, push wave with Q then look to roam bot or top. Against melee assassins, poke with autos and Q at max range. Rush Lost Chapter first back for mana sustain. Luden's Companion is the standard mythic for burst and waveclear.

## Mid Game Roaming (15-25 min)
Ahri hits a massive power spike at Luden's + Sorc Shoes. Look for picks in enemy jungle with sweeper — Charm from fog of war into full combo kills most squishies. Your R gives 3 dashes for safe engages and escapes. Coordinate roams with your jungler for 2v1 dives on side lanes. Prioritize bot lane roams to snowball your ADC. For dragon fights, stay on the edge and fish for Charms — one pick pre-fight is often a won objective. Keep mid wave pushed before rotating. Build Shadowflame second against squishies, or Zhonya's against AD threats.

## Late Game Strategy (25+ min)
In late game, Ahri transitions from assassin to utility pick-maker. Flash-Charm can win the game if it catches enemy carry. Stay on flanks or behind walls where enemies don't have vision. Your R resets on takedown — use it to reposition after initial engage. Don't die for a Charm attempt. Play front-to-back if you can't flank. Build Rabadon's Deathcap for raw AP or Void Staff if enemy stacks MR. Zhonya's active lets you engage with R-W then go stasis while team follows up.

## Decision Making
- Pre-objective fights: sweep enemy jungle 30s before dragon/baron spawn, look for picks
- When behind: clear waves safely and peel for your ADC with Charm
- When ahead: pressure side lanes and force picks in enemy jungle
- Late game: Do NOT facecheck bushes without R up
- Gold income: prioritize CS over risky roams if you're even or behind

## Core Items and Build Order
Luden's Companion → Sorcerer's Shoes → Shadowflame → Zhonya's Hourglass → Rabadon's Deathcap → Void Staff

## Important Matchups
- Zed: rush Zhonya's first item. Charm behind you when he ults (he appears behind you). Respect level 6 all-in.
- Fizz: hard counter. Rush Banshee's Veil or Zhonya's. Respect his Q-AA-W-E trade. Don't push without jungler nearby.
- Yone: keep distance and poke. Charm him when he uses E forward (his spirit form returns after). Sidestep his Q3.
- Syndra: skill matchup. Dodge Q pokes, all-in at 6 with R. Build Merc Treads early.
- Sylas: dodge his E2 (chain). Poke with Q when his E is on cooldown. Respect his level 3.
