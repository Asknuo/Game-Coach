# Lee Sin - Jungle Guide

## Early Game Clear (0-8 min)
Standard clear path: Red → Raptors → Krugs → gank bot or mid. Use W on yourself for sustain between camps. Hold second Q cast until monster is low to maximize execute damage. Look for level 3 gank with Q-Flash-W combo for surprise engages. Invade enemy jungle if you have lane priority and spot enemy jungler on opposite side. Lee Sin is strongest at level 3-6, force early fights.

## Mid Game Objective Control (8-20 min)
Prioritize Herald at 8 minutes if bot lane is winning. For dragon fights, ward enemy jungle entrances 45s before spawn and look for Insec kick (Ward-W-R) to knock enemy jungler away from pit. Use Q-Smite-Q combo to secure objectives. Counter-gank by tracking enemy jungler and shadowing pushed lanes. Build Eclipse first for dueling, then Black Cleaver if enemy has armor.

## Late Game Teamfighting (20+ min)
Lee Sin falls off late game. Focus on peeling for your carries with R instead of diving backline. Use kick to disengage or isolate priority targets. Ward-W-R combo can singlehandedly win fights if you catch enemy ADC. Do NOT engage first unless you have GA or Zhonya's. Build Guardian Angel as 3rd/4th item. Look for flanks rather than front-to-back fights.

## Core Build
Eclipse → Plated Steelcaps/Mercury Treads → Black Cleaver → Death's Dance → Guardian Angel → Maw of Malmortius

## Key Combos
- Insec: Q1 → Q2 → Ward → W → R (kick enemy into team)
- Chinese Insec: Ward → W → Flash → R → Q1 → Q2 (follow kick)
- Objective secure: Q1 → Smite → Q2
- Basic trade: W to ward/minion → E1 → auto → E2 → Q1 → Q2

## Matchups to Avoid
Nidalee: outpaces you, avoid 1v1, countergank instead. Kha'Zix: can 1v1 you if isolated, do not fight in river alone. Rek'Sai: her tunnel and knockup counter your mobility, respect her early.
