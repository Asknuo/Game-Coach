# Aatrox - Top Lane Guide

## Early Game Laning (0-14 min)
Play aggressive level 1-3 with Q poke. Zone enemy off CS using Q1-Q2 threat range. Save E for repositioning Q sweet spots. Start with Doran's Blade against melee, Doran's Shield against ranged poke. Rush Eclipse or Profane Hydra first item. Ward river at 2:45 to avoid early jungle ganks. Against tanks, take Conqueror and look for extended trades where you can stack it. Freeze wave near your turret if behind, push and roam mid if ahead. Use passive auto on enemy champion whenever it's up for sustain.

## Mid Game Skirmishing (15-25 min)
Aatrox power spike at Eclipse + tier 2 boots. Look for TP flanks behind enemy team at dragon or herald fights. Your ult gives massive healing — engage when enemies commit their CC. Use Q1-E forward into Q2 sweet spot combo to chunk squishies. In teamfights, hit frontline with Q1 to stack Conqueror, then E-Q3 into backline. Build Death's Dance second for survivability, or Sterak's Gage against burst. Push side lanes and pressure turrets, then rotate to objectives with TP advantage.

## Late Game Teamfighting (25+ min)
Aatrox is a strong teamfighter if not focused. Stay with team and use Q from medium range. Do NOT be the primary engage — let support or jungle go first. Your ult healing is countered by Grievous Wounds, so avoid fighting when ignited. Build Spirit Visage as 3rd/4th item to amplify ult healing. In late game, protecting your ADC with Q knockups can be more valuable than diving. Look for flank angles with TP in late game baron fights.

## Core Build
Eclipse → Plated Steelcaps → Death's Dance → Sterak's Gage → Spirit Visage → Guardian Angel

## Key Combos
- Fast trade: Q1 → E → Q2 → E → Q3 sweet spot
- All-in: R → Q1 → E forward → W → Q2 → Q3
- Kite back: Q1 backwards → E backwards → Q2 → W to peel
- Quick poke: Q1-E forward to hit sweet spot then walk away

## Bad Matchups
Fiora: dodge W parry, do NOT Q3 into her, trade when her W is down. Irelia: respect her full stacks, freeze wave near turret, build Bramble Vest early. Kled: his Q grievous wounds counters your healing, play safe levels 1-5.
